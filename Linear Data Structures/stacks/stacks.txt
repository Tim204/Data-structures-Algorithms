STACKS

Uses:
    . Stacks are usually used to implement undo features.
    . Can be used for building compilers( for parsing the syntax of an expression )
    . Evaluate arithmetic expressions.
    . Build navigation apps or web browsers (forward/back)

. Stacks are based around the LIFO(Last In, First Out) principal

NOTE: Whenever you deal with a problem involving doing an action in reverse, STACKS are Your best friend!
      . You could take reversing a string as an example.
